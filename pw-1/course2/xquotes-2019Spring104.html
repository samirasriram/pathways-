Topics of this class:
The real number system. Sequences, limits, and continuous functions. Metric spaces. Uniform convergence, interchange of limit operations. Infinite series. Mean value theorem and applications. The Riemann integral.

Lectures: (updated weekly)
Week 1: Introduction to mathematical proofs. Handouts courtesy of Michael Hutchings
Week 2: Real numbers, completeness, infinity (Section 3-5)
Week 3: Sequences and limits (Section 7-9)
Week 4: Monotone sequences, Cauchy sequences, lim sup and lim inf, subsequences (Section 10-11)
Week 5: Lim sup/lim inf, Series, convergent tests (Section 14-15)
Week 6: Continuous functions (Section 17-18)
Week 7: Properties of continuous functions, uniform continuous functions (Section 18-19)
Week 8: (Uniform) convergence of functions. (Section 24)
Week 9: Review and midterm.
Week 10: Power series and uniform convergence (Section 23, 25-26)
Week 11: Differentiation, Mean Value Theorem (Section 28-29)
Week 12: Taylor's theorem, Integrals, Propoerties of integrals (Section 31-33)
Week 13: Properties of integrals, Fundamental theorem of calculus (Section 33-34)
Week 14: Review.
